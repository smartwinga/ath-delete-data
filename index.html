Delete My Data - ATH Online Doctors App

Welcome to the ATH Online Doctors App! If you wish to delete your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the ATH Online Doctors App on your device.
  2. Tap on the Profile icon located at the bottom right corner of the screen.
  3. In the Profile menu, tap on "Settings/Delete Acc."
  4. Within the "Delete Account" section, follow the provided instructions to permanently delete your account and data.

Please be aware that deleting your account and data is irreversible, and you will lose all your information associated with the app. If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our support team.
